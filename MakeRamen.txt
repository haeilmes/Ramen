How to make instant ramen
1. Pick flavor of ramen packet
2. Grab cooking pot where ever you keep stored in kitchen
3. Pure water into cooking pot
4. wait for wait for water to come to a rolling boil
5. open ramen packet
6. Carefully place water into boiling water  
7. Wait for ramen to become fully cooked and tender
8. Grab Strainer and place in sink
	a. carefully pour cooking pot into strainer
9. Place fully cooked ramen into bowl
10. Add seasoning included in ramen packet (ref to Step 1.)
11. Grab a fork where its kept in kitchen
	a. or chopsticks 
12. Let cool (Burning the roof of your mouth is no bueno)
13. ENJOY your instant ramen
